Bullseye Performance Tracker is a mobile app for serious target shooting enthusiasts looking to improve their shooting performance.

Every target shooter knows that the key to consistent performance is to practice under repeatable circumstances and understand how those conditions effect their equipment.

It's time to replace the marked-up paper scraps, napkins, and ammo boxes with a real, safe, and reliable alternative to record your shooting and performance data from the comfort of your mobile phone. Introducing the Bullseye Performance Tracker mobile platform app!

Bullseye Performance Tracker is superior to existing shooting apps. It is built by shooters, for shooters. Not only does it offer an excellent mobile interface, it also provides an optional PC interface software to further analyse data, upload stats to Google Docs, and generate complex graphs showing correlation of conditions to performance.

How does Bullseye Performance Tracker help?

  • Track performance based on ammunition brand per firearm
  • Record your grain weights for reloaded rounds
  • Compare performance data per firearm with various ammunition
  • Chart performance based on environmental conditions
  • Log scores based on range location, shooting position, and different firearms
  • Built-in tools to assist in range and wind estimation
  • IPSC scoring and performance tracking

FAQ

Why $60,000?
An upfront investment of $50,000 will cover the yearly salary cost of two software developers ($50,000pa) and administrative expenses for a 6 month period.
Will I be able to add my own tracking categories?
Yes. The initial software release will allow up to three custom categories that the user can define and track across their activity periods.
Will using Bullseye Performance Tracker guarantee improvement to my shooting skills?
The only thing that can improve your shooting skills is dedication and practice. Sadly, there's no quick fix. Although, having an awesome mobile app to help you is definitely a plus!
